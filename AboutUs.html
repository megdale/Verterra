About Us

Verterra is Australia's first full service Ecological Engineering Company. Verterra has integrated capabilities in research, consulting and operational management, specialising in vegetation, land and water management for delivery of environmental services and production of bio-renewables from managed and natural vegetation.

Verterra was founded in 1999 as a specialist technical advisory consultancy focussing on technology, production systems and intellectual property in the forestry, agricultural and natural resource management sectors. The company was restructured in 2008, and has since grown rapidly to provide integrated research, consulting and operational management services. Verterra specialises in providing vegetation, land and water management services, to a wide range of industry sectors including oil and gas, mining, energy, power, water resources, forestry and agriculture.


Services Delivery:

Consulting services Verterra focuses on providing consulting services as a tailored solution package. We aim to understand the attributes of the issues faced by our clients, identify the technically and practically viable solutions, then narrow down a sub-set of solutions that meet technical constraints, our clients’ objectives, and fit the wider community and land-use context. Verterra can also support our clients’ operational needs by providing technical, land management and environmental services as individual service components.
Turnkey project delivery Verterra has the capacity, expertise experience and accreditations to provide an end-to-end project delivery service from conceptual studies, through feasibility, design, regulatory approval, procurement and construction, to commissioning ongoing operation, monitoring and review. Verterra’s highly experienced project delivery staff and systems aim to delight clients by ensuring seamless, hassle-free project delivery.
Project management Verterra has highly developed project management and business systems that are applicable to planning, procurement, implementation, quality control, risk management and commissioning of any large complex project. Verterra’s project management systems ensure on-time, on-budget delivery of quality project outcomes in a safe working environment.
Research services Verterra staff and associates include highly skilled, widely published scientists with considerable experience in research aimed at developing practical solutions to commercial problems.
Value engineering Value engineering is directed at improving performance, reliability, quality, safety, and life cycle costs. Value engineering achieves this aim through a systematic process of analysing the function or objective of a process, and identifying opportunities for improvement. We aim to work in close partnership with our clients to deliver value by reducing cost and improving performance.
Build, own, operate In combination with our alliance partners, Verterra can provide services on a full build-own-operate basis for green infrastructure and renewables projects. We aim to provide our clients with the advantage of focussing attention, effort and capital on core business, while outsourcing the design, construction, ownership, operation and maintenance functions associated with implementation of green infrastructure projects such as waste-water management, carbon bio-sequestration offsets, bio-energy feed stocks and renewable resource supplies.
Personnel secondment Verterra can provide specialist staff on secondment for key projects, providing our clients with flexible, cost-effective access to specialist technical skills, supported by Verterra’s wider pool of specialist technical knowledge and expertise.
